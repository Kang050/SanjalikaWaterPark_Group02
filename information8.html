HA LONG LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANY BOND ISSUANCE PLAN

8 November, 2019 /
/

Ha Long Limited Liability company discloses the bond issuance plan, with the summary information as follows:

+ Bond name: HLC112020
+ Total value of bonds expected to be issued: 700 billions maximum
+ Expected issued date: No later than 30/11/2019
+ Bond term: 1 year 1 day
+ Face value: 100.000 VND/bond
+ Issued method: Issued separately, following the Decree No. 163/2018/ND-CP in 04/12/2019 of the Government of the Corporate bond issuance
+ Type of bond: Non-convertible bonds, not accompanied by warrants, guaranteed with assets.
+ Bond interest rates: The interest rate is a combination of a fixed rate and a floating rate
