CORPORATE BOND ISSUANCE PLAN OF HA LONG SUN LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANY

8 November, 2019 /
/

Ha Long Sun Limited Liability Company announced information about corporate bond issuance plan with summary information as follows:

+ Name of Bond: HLC112020

+ Estimated total value of bonds issued: Maximum VND 2,000 billion

+ Expected issuance date: No later than December 31, 2019 (minimum 10 working days after Information disclosure date November 7)

+ Bond term: 1 year 1 day

+ Face value: VND 100,000/Bond

+ Method of issuing: Issuing privately under Decree No. 163/2018/ND-CP dated December 4, 2018 of the Government on issuing corporate bonds

+ Type of bonds: Bonds are not convertible, not accompanied by warrants, and are guaranteed with assets.

+ Bond interest rate: Interest rate is a combination of fixed and floating interest rates
